STATIC ASSETS — WHAT TO PUT HERE
=================================

resume.pdf
  Place your resume PDF here as resume.pdf.
  It is referenced in data/contact.ts as resumeUrl: "/Ahmed-Shahid-Portfolio/resume.pdf"
  and is linked from both the Hero social row and the Contact page.

og-image.png
  Replace with your Open Graph image.
  Recommended size: 1200 x 630 px.
  Referenced in app/layout.tsx metadata.openGraph.images.
  Used by LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and iMessage link previews.

favicon.ico / icon.png
  Next.js auto-detects app/favicon.ico or app/icon.png for the browser tab icon.
  Place your favicon in the app/ directory (not here).
